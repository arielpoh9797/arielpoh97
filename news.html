New stand opened in Nottingshamshire

10-5-2019 Sunday / Nottinghamshire street
A fresh fruit and veg stall has opened outside the main entrance of a Nottinghamshire hospital. The new stand opened Kings Mill Hospital in Sutton-in-Ashfield on Monday, January 7. The decision to add the stall came from Sherwood Forest Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust, which operates the site, in a bid to encourage healthy eating for staff, visitors and patients at Kings Mill Hospital. Laiten McGuigan and Roxanne Burrows, who are both from Leicester, will be running the stall which will be open Monday to Friday, from 8.30am until 4.30pm and will accept both cash and card payments.
